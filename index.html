Betting with TDT Picks
Data-led betting built for long-term profit
⚠️ This is not a tipster service built on guesswork or hype.

We use data, performance tracking, pricing edges, and deeper match analysis to uncover profitable opportunities over the long term.

Our approach combines traditional value betting with normal bets that are included because of strong, well-analysed positions. Some picks will be clear value where the odds are bigger than the true probability, while others are selected because the overall data and analysis point towards a long-term edge.

In other words, you do not need every bet to look like obvious “value” on paper to be profitable. Long-term success can come from a mix of value bets and well-analysed positions where the edge is in our favour.

We have been working on and refining this approach for over 3 years behind the scenes before creating this app.

We only post bets that we have already staked ourselves. You can track everything using your own My Tracker, while the TDT Tracker shows every official bet we have placed and the results since the app has been running.

For long-term profitability, the best approach is to follow as singles. Accas are your choice, but they come with higher risk and more variance.

The goal is consistent, long-term profit, not short-term hype. The VIP preview box at the bottom shows how much has been made since the app has been running, based on example £2.50 stakes. This is set low by default so even users who prefer smaller stakes can clearly see performance and get a realistic idea of how the strategy performs over time.

If you choose to stake higher amounts, the profit will naturally scale up, but the overall approach and edge remain the same.

Expect variance and swings during the process — this is completely normal, even with a profitable strategy. There will be periods of losses and drawdowns, but over time the edge plays out. The focus should always remain on long-term performance, not short-term results.

Always stake responsibly. We advise between 0.5% and 1% of your bankroll as a safer range, and no more than 2% maximum on any one bet.
